# 03 Category and Attribute System

## Ten Top-Level Categories
Electronics & Technology; Home & Kitchen; Pet Products; Beauty & Personal Care; Outdoor & Sports; Fashion & Accessories; Baby & Kids; Automotive; Office & Productivity; Lifestyle & Gifts.

## Category Data
Hierarchy, description, hero image, SEO intro, order and status.

## Attribute Engine
Field types: text, number, dropdown, radio, checkbox, multi-select, boolean and range.

Examples:
Electronics: compatibility, connectivity, battery life, warranty.  
Pet: pet type, age group, material, safety feature.

Use standardized selectable values where filtering/comparison matters; use free text only when necessary.

## Category Page
Hero; introduction; subcategories; filters; featured brands; brand list; guides; FAQ.

## Acceptance
Hierarchy and attributes are admin-configurable without code changes; forms adapt by category; filters and cross-category brands work.
