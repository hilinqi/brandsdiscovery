# 10 Settings and Integrations

## General
Site identity, defaults, search, submission and claim settings.

## R2
Secure endpoint/bucket/custom-domain configuration, credentials outside committed code, image presets and upload test. Store logos, covers, product, category and guide images.

## Email
Use SMTP provider through WordPress mail. Registration, reset, claim and submission notifications.

## Social Login
Third-party OAuth creates/links WordPress users; business modules use WordPress roles/capabilities only.

## Cookie Consent
Use mature consent plugin.

## Security
Capability and nonce checks; secrets hidden and never committed.

## Acceptance
R2 upload/display, email, social login, consent and protected settings all work.
