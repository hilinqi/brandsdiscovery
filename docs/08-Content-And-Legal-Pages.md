# 08 Content and Legal Pages

## Content Types
Buying Guide, Best Brand List, Brand Review, Comparison and General Article.

## Required Pages
About, Contact, FAQ, Editorial Policy, Verification Policy, Privacy Policy, Terms, Cookie Policy, Affiliate Disclosure, Advertise With Us, Partner With Us and Submit Your Brand.

## Rules
English only; useful to consumers; sponsored content disclosed; paid placement never presented as independent editorial ranking.

## Third-Party V1
Mature plugins may handle cookie consent, forms, SMTP and social login. Integrations must remain replaceable.

## Acceptance
All pages exist; footer/navigation links work; terms acceptance and cookie preferences work; sponsorship disclosures display.
